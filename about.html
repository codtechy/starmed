About Us

what is Ozak LLC- FZ

Ozak LLC-FZ is a diversified and dynamic Free Zone company established with a vision to deliver high quality services and products across various sectors. With a strong focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Ozak LLC-FZ operates in multiple business verticals including electrical maintenance, general trading, and e-commerce.
We serve a broad spectrum of clients by offering reliable electrical repair solutions and supplying a wide range of consumer and industrial products through both traditional and digital retail platforms.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional electrical repair services and a wide range of quality products to meet the evolving needs of our customers across all channels—traditional and digital.

Our Vision

To be a trusted namein electrical services & global trading, known for integrity, innovation, & customer - centric solutions.
